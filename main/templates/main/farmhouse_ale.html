Farmhouse ale is an ancient European tradition where
farmers brewed beer for consumption on the farm from
their own grain. Most farmers would brew for Christmas
and/or the late summer work, but in areas where they had
enough grain farmers would use beer as the every-day
drink.This was in a time when it was safer to
drink beer than water.Farmhouse ale has enormous
variation in the ingredients and brewing process used,
both of which follow ancient local traditions.
